Conclave 1.1/com.r3.conclave.mail/PostOffice/decryptMail

decryptMail

EnclaveMail decryptMail(byte[] encryptedEnclaveMail)
Content copied to clipboard

Decodes and decrypts the mail with senderPrivateKey and verifies that the authenticated sender (EnclaveMail.authenticatedSender) matches the destinationPublicKey.

Parameters

encryptedEnclaveMail

The encrypted mail bytes, produced by the sender's encryptMail.

Throws

java.lang.IllegalArgumentException

If the mail is not targeted to the destination public key.

java.io.IOException

if the mail is malformed or corrupted in some way.
